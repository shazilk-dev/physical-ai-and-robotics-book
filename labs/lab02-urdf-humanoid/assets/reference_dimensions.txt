Reference Dimensions for Lab 2 Humanoid URDF
==============================================

Link Dimensions:
----------------

Torso (base link):
- Shape: Box
- Size: 0.3m × 0.2m × 0.4m (W × D × H)
- Mass: 5.0 kg
- Origin: Center at (0, 0, 0.2) - 0.2m above ground

Head:
- Shape: Sphere
- Radius: 0.075m (7.5cm)
- Mass: 1.0 kg
- Mounted at: (0, 0, 0.45) on top of torso

Left Upper Arm:
- Shape: Cylinder
- Radius: 0.03m (3cm)
- Length: 0.25m
- Mass: 1.0 kg
- Origin: Cylinder center at (0, 0, -0.125)

Left Forearm:
- Shape: Cylinder
- Radius: 0.025m (2.5cm, slightly thinner)
- Length: 0.25m
- Mass: 0.8 kg
- Origin: Cylinder center at (0, 0, -0.125)

Right Arm (mirror of left):
- Same dimensions as left arm
- Mounted on opposite side (Y-coordinate negated)

Joint Positions:
----------------

Neck:
- Parent: torso
- Child: head
- Position: (0, 0, 0.45) - top of torso

Left Shoulder:
- Parent: torso
- Child: left_upper_arm (via virtual link)
- Position: (0, 0.15, 0.35) - left side of upper torso

Right Shoulder:
- Parent: torso
- Child: right_upper_arm (via virtual link)
- Position: (0, -0.15, 0.35) - right side of upper torso

Left Elbow:
- Parent: left_upper_arm
- Child: left_forearm
- Position: (0, 0, -0.25) - end of upper arm

Right Elbow:
- Parent: right_upper_arm
- Child: right_forearm
- Position: (0, 0, -0.25) - end of upper arm

Total Robot Reach:
------------------
- Arm length: 0.25m (upper) + 0.25m (forearm) = 0.5m per arm
- Total width (arms extended): ~1.3m (0.15 shoulder offset × 2 + 0.5m arm × 2)
- Height: 0.45m (torso top) + 0.075m (head radius) ≈ 0.53m
